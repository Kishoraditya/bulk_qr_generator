About QR Code Generator

What is a QR Code?

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can be scanned using smartphone cameras to quickly access information, websites, or perform actions.

Features of Our QR Code Generator

  • Bulk Generation: Create multiple QR codes at once from Excel or CSV data
  • Custom Styling: Choose from various styles including standard, rounded, and gradient QR codes
  • vCard Generation: Create QR codes that add contact information directly to a phone
  • Image Embedding: Combine QR codes with logos or images for branded codes
  • Multiple Formats: Download as individual PNG files or as a formatted PDF

Use Cases

QR codes are versatile and can be used for:

  • Business cards and contact information
  • Product packaging and inventory management
  • Event tickets and check-ins
  • Restaurant menus and ordering systems
  • Marketing campaigns and promotional materials
  • Educational resources and interactive learning

QR Code Tips

  • Test your QR codes before distributing
  • Use higher error correction for printed codes
  • Ensure adequate contrast for better scanning
  • Include a call-to-action near your QR code
  • Keep URLs short for simpler QR patterns
Did You Know?

QR codes can store up to 4,296 alphanumeric characters or 7,089 numeric digits!
